Home

I am a Research Economist at the Federal Reserve Bank of Dallas. My research builds on models of frictional markets with sound micro-foundations. Until now, I have studied their application to monetary theory, over-the-counter financial markets, labor markets, and more recently, to the spread of a virus through social interactions.

Feel free to contact me at lucielebeau.econ@gmail.com.
